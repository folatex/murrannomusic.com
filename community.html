GTKM
“Get to Know Me” is not your typical podcast; it’s a front-row seat to the vibrant world of emerging artists. Picture this – a live session where the spotlight shifts from the melodies to the makers. Now, take that energy and excitement and channel it into a weekly podcast that invites you to delve deeper into the lives and stories of diverse up-and-coming talents. From indie rock to hip-hop beats, each episode unravels the unique journey of a different artist, offering listeners an intimate connection beyond the music. “Get to Know Me” transforms the live session experience into a personal conversation, providing insights, inspirations, and candid moments that bring you closer to the artists shaping the future of the music industry.
